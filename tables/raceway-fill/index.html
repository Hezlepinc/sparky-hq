Fill Percentages
1 conductor = 53% fill allowed. 2 conductors = 31% fill. 3 or more conductors = 40% fill. These percentages come from NEC Chapter 9, Table 1.
Mixed Wire Sizes?
This chart is for same-size conductors only. For mixed wire sizes, use the Conduit Fill Calculator which computes actual cross-sectional areas.

Values shown are for THHN/THWN insulation type. Other insulation types have different outer diameters and will yield different fill counts.
