Important
Most terminations are rated 75°C. Use the 75°C column unless you've verified the terminal rating on both ends. The 90°C column is commonly used only as a starting point for derating calculations per NEC 310.15(B).
Derating Reminder
These values assume no more than 3 current-carrying conductors in raceway or cable. For 4–6 conductors, derate to 80%. For 7–9, derate to 70%. See NEC 310.15(C)(1).
