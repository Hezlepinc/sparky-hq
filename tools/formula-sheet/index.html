Electrical Formula Cheat Sheet Quick Reference

Essential electrical formulas in one place. Print-friendly layout for your toolbox.

Ohm's Law

  • V = I × R
  • I = V / R
  • R = V / I
  • P = V × I
  • P = I² × R
  • P = V² / R

Single-Phase Power

  • kVA = V × I / 1000
  • kW = kVA × PF
  • I = kVA × 1000 / V
  • I = kW × 1000 / (V × PF)
  • kW = V × I × PF / 1000

Three-Phase Power

  • kVA = V × I × 1.732 / 1000
  • kW = kVA × PF
  • I = kVA × 1000 / (V × 1.732)
  • I = kW × 1000 / (V × 1.732 × PF)
  • kW = V × I × 1.732 × PF / 1000

Motor Formulas

  • HP = kW / 0.746
  • kW = HP × 0.746
  • Efficiency = Output / Input × 100
  • Torque (lb-ft) = HP × 5252 / RPM
  • Sync RPM = 120 × Freq / Poles
  • Slip = (Sync RPM - Actual) / Sync RPM

FLA: Use NEC Tables 430.247–430.250 for motor full-load current, not nameplate.

Transformer Formulas

  • kVA = V × I / 1000 (1φ)
  • kVA = V × I × 1.732 / 1000 (3φ)
  • I = kVA × 1000 / V (1φ)
  • I = kVA × 1000 / (V × 1.732) (3φ)
  • Turns Ratio = Vpri / Vsec
  • Vpri × Ipri = Vsec × Isec

NEC Quick Rules

  • Continuous load: 125% (210.20, 215.3)
  • Motor conductor: 125% FLC (430.22)
  • Motor OCPD: varies by type (430.52)
  • Voltage drop: 3% branch, 5% total (210.19 Info Note)

Standard breaker sizes (240.6):
15, 20, 25, 30, 35, 40, 45, 50, 60, 70, 80, 90, 100, 110, 125, 150, 175, 200, 225, 250, 300, 350, 400, 450, 500, 600

Conduit & Box Fill

  • 1 wire: 53% fill (Ch.9 Table 1)
  • 2 wires: 31% fill (Ch.9 Table 1)
  • 3+ wires: 40% fill (Ch.9 Table 1)
  • Nipple (≤24"): 60% fill (Ch.9 Note 4)

Box fill (314.16): Each conductor = 1× largest conductor volume. Clamps = 1×. Devices = 2×. EGC = 1× largest.
