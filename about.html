About Sparky HQ

Sparky HQ is a collection of free electrical calculators built for electricians, estimators, and engineers who need quick NEC-compliant answers on the job site or at the desk.

Every tool is based on current NEC requirements and built from real-world experience in the trade. No ads, no signup, no app to download. Open the page, run your calculation, get back to work.

Sparky HQ is a project by Hezlepinc.
